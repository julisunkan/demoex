MailVault Pro does NOT sell subscriptions through Microsoft AppSource. All
billing (USDT payments and license keys) is handled entirely inside the app
via your own admin portal — there is no Microsoft SaaS Fulfillment offer,
no Azure AD app registration, and no Microsoft billing integration.

This file is only useful if you choose to ALSO list MailVault Pro on
AppSource / the Office Store as a free discovery listing, purely so users
can find and install the add-in from within Outlook's "Get Add-ins"
dialog. The listing is free to submit, requires no pricing plans, no
webhook, and no landing page token exchange — it simply points to your
hosted manifest.xml. Users still unlock Pro the same way regardless of how
they installed the add-in: by paying with USDT or entering a license key.

If you don't need Office Store discovery, skip AppSource entirely and just
distribute manifest.xml directly (via Centralized Deployment or a
sideloading link) — see DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.txt Part 5.

--- PLAIN TEXT VERSION ---

MailVault Pro brings enterprise-grade email backup, restore, and analytics
directly into Outlook — no external portal, no separate app to manage.

Install it in seconds, and your first backup runs in under two minutes.
No Microsoft sign-in and no account creation required to get started.

FREE FEATURES (no license needed)
• Back up 1 mailbox
• Local storage backups
• Manual, on-demand backups
• Basic mailbox analytics
• Community support

PRO FEATURES (unlock with USDT payment or license key)
• Unlimited mailboxes
• Store backups on Local, Azure Blob, OneDrive, Amazon S3, Google Drive,
  or Dropbox — configure multiple destinations simultaneously
• AES-256-GCM encryption on every backup, before it ever leaves your device
• Automated daily, weekly, or monthly backup scheduling
• AI-powered mailbox analytics — volume trends, largest senders, folder
  sizes, storage growth, and projected cleanup savings
• Multi-user and group support for teams
• Custom retention policies with automated pruning
• Advanced audit logs
• Priority support

SIMPLE, TRANSPARENT LICENSING
Unlike typical Office add-ins, MailVault Pro does not lock you into a
Microsoft-billed subscription. Pay once with USDT (Tron or Ethereum
network) and get a license key automatically, or enter a license key
provided directly by your organization or reseller. No recurring
Microsoft charges, no forced sign-in.

ONE-CLICK RESTORE
Restore individual emails, entire folders, or your complete mailbox from
any backup point. Preview before restoring — no accidental overwrites.

SMART CLEANUP
Safely delete old newsletters, duplicates, and promotional emails, with a
full preview before anything is permanently removed.

CENTRALIZED IT DEPLOYMENT
IT admins can push MailVault Pro to their entire organization via the
Microsoft 365 Admin Center's Centralized Deployment — zero user action
required, and no marketplace purchase involved.

GETTING STARTED
1. Install the add-in (via Office Store or a direct manifest link from
   your IT admin)
2. Open any email in Outlook — MailVault Pro appears in the task pane
3. Run your first backup — it takes less than two minutes
4. When ready, pay with USDT or enter a license key to unlock Pro features

Pro pricing starts at $9.99/month, with an annual plan available at a
17% discount. Exact pricing is set by your organization's MailVault Pro
admin portal and may vary.
