--- PLAIN TEXT VERSION ---

Financial Data Analyzer turns Microsoft Excel into a powerful personal finance
dashboard — no formulas, no pivot tables, no manual work required.

Open the add-in, point it at your spreadsheet or paste a CSV export from your
bank portal, and get an instant breakdown of where your money is going.

WORKS IN SECONDS
Financial Data Analyzer automatically detects your date, description, and
amount columns — no configuration required. It supports debit/credit column
formats, single-amount column formats, and Excel serial date values.

FREE FEATURES (no subscription needed)
• Analyze transactions from any bank or financial institution
• Auto-categorize into 14 categories: Salary, Groceries, Food & Dining,
  Transport, Fuel, Utilities, Shopping, Entertainment, Healthcare, Savings,
  Investments, Loans, Education, and Rent & Housing
• Monthly income vs. expense trend chart
• Financial health score with personalized improvement tips
• Month-over-month category spending comparison
• Search, filter by category, type, and amount range
• Top 5 largest individual expenses at a glance
• Average daily and monthly spending stats
• Income sources breakdown by category
• Top merchants ranked by total spend
• Flag transactions for manual review
• Recategorize any transaction with one tap

PRO FEATURES (AppSource subscription required)
• Highlight Excel cells by spending category with automatic color coding
• Export a formatted Excel summary sheet directly to your workbook
• Download a categorized CSV file for further analysis in any tool
• Export a color-coded PDF report, or email it directly from the add-in
• Recurring subscription detector — automatically finds repeat charges
• Duplicate transaction flagging with one-click review
• Category budget tracker — set monthly budgets and track spending vs. target
• Tax deduction tracker — tag transactions as tax-deductible and get your
  total deductible amount instantly
• Cash flow projection — estimates next month's income and expenses based on
  your historical monthly averages
• Full merchant spending report

YOUR DATA NEVER LEAVES EXCEL
All financial analysis happens locally inside your Excel session. No
transaction data, bank credentials, or personal financial information is ever
uploaded to any server. Your data stays completely private.

PERFECT FOR
• Personal monthly budget reviews
• Tax preparation and deduction tracking
• Freelancers and self-employed tracking income and business expenses
• Business expense analysis and reporting
• Anyone who wants to understand their spending without leaving Excel

GETTING STARTED
1. Install the add-in from Microsoft AppSource or the Excel Add-ins store
2. Open any Excel sheet containing your bank data (or paste CSV text)
3. Click "Analyze Active Sheet" — results appear in seconds
4. Upgrade to Pro on AppSource to unlock export, budgets, and more

Pro plans start at $5/month. Annual plans available at a discount.
